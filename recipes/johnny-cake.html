Johnny cake

Description

Breakfast johnny cakes are delicious when split and buttered immediately. Smaller cakes, filled with cream cheese, may be served at tea time.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 tsp. butter
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 2 tsp. sugar

Directions:

  1. Sift 2 cups of flour before measuring
  2. Re-sift it with 2 tsp. baking powder and 1 tsp. salt.
  3. Using two knives or a pastry blender, cut 2 tsp of butter into the flour
  4. Combine 1/3 cup water and 2 tsp. sugar and stir until the sugar is dissolved
  5. To make the dough, add the liquid a little at a time to the flour.
  6. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead it well.
  7. Pinch off pieces, roll them into small balls and flatten into circles two inches in diameter and one-fourth inch thick.
  8. Or roll out dough to the same thickness and cut circles with a cookie cutter.
  9. Fry the cakes in hot fat until they are puffy and golden. (A cast-iron skillet is ideal for this, but it must be kept heavily greased by adding oil from time to time while the cakes are frying).
  10. Drain the cakes on absorbent paper and keep them warm in the oven.
  11. Breakfast johnny cakes are delicious when split and buttered immediately.
  12. Smaller cakes, filled with cream cheese, may be served at tea time.
